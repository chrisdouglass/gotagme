Frequently Asked Questions

What is GoTag.Me?

We know, you're probably thinking "ugh, not another social network!" And we agree with you. There are so many websites out there already for posting photos and socializing. But this is different, we promise. It's not a gallery in the traditional sense. There's nothing to upload, and no need to scour your contact list and add your friends yet again. Instead, it's a simple service for finding and collecting photos of your and your friends' fursuits, so everyone can find all of their favorites in one place.

Okay... but how does it work?

It's simple! When you find a photo of a fursuiter you know, use our submission form or bookmarklet to send it to us, along with the Twitter @names of any fursuiters pictured - you don't even need an account. We'll take those links and send them along to the fursuiters who've been tagged.

Once you link your Twitter account to GoTag.Me, any photos where you've been tagged will be waiting for you to approve and add to your character collections. The end result is a centralized repository for all the great photos of your suit(s) that live all over the internet, all sorted by owner and character, with handy links back to their original locations.

What's the catch?

Well, it's one more thing in your life to keep track of. But we like to think we're solving a problem, and we hope you'll give it a try and agree.
