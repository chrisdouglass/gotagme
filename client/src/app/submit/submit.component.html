Submit Photos

So you found some photos of a fursuiter you know? Awesome!

  1. Paste a link to the photo below (multiple links are okay, as long as they're all of the same group)
  2. Add the Twitter @names for the fursuiters pictured.
  3. We'll let those people know they've been tagged in a new photo.
  4. Once they approve the photo it'll be added to their collection!
Want a faster way? Grab the GoTag.Me bookmarklet and just click when you find a photo you want to submit!
